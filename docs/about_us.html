Team Trailblazers

We are Team Trailblazers who are currently working on the project "Eat healthy, live better" with the topic "Food choices and our carbon footprint". Trailblazers consist of five members ranging from all IT disciplines at Monash University. We are proposing the first web application that connects diet recommendations with carbon footprint! As Australia is the second-largest per capita consumption of meat and dairy in the world, we are committed to helping people in Australia take action on climate change. Our project aims to improve Australian residents' awareness of eating clean by integrating relevant information and recommending eco-friendly recipes!
